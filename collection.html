Packaging Standards

All our blooms—whether standard or exotic—are packed to rigorous B2B standards to survive transit and arrive event-ready.

  • Hydration Sleeves: Essential stems are shipped with individual hydration sources.
  • Corrugated B2B Boxes: Heavy-duty protection against crushing and temperature shifts.
  • Pre-Cooled Transit: Flowers are forced-air cooled before loading into refrigerated transport.

Why Choose Our Bulk Flowers?

Fleur Vine specializes in delivering premium, farm-fresh bulk flowers designed specifically for grand weddings, mandap decorations, corporate events, and large-scale celebrations across India. We understand that event planners and decorators require absolute reliability, consistent grading, and vibrant colors that hold up under event lighting.

Whether you need thousands of stems of Avalanche roses for a sprawling floral ceiling or exotic orchids to create a stunning entryway, our wholesale collection is meticulously curated to meet the demanding standards of the B2B floral industry. By sourcing directly from top-tier farms and maintaining a strict cold-chain process, we guarantee that every stem arrives at your venue with maximum vase life and uncompromising beauty.
