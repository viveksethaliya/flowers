Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)?

Our minimum order quantity is 500 stems per variety, to ensure we can provide the best wholesale pricing and dedicate the proper cold-chain logistics to your order.

How much notice is required?

We request at least 7-10 days' notice for standard bulk orders. For specialty or exotic imports, we recommend reaching out 3-4 weeks in advance of your event date.

Do you deliver outside major cities?

Yes! We utilize a robust network of road and air transport to deliver to almost any venue in India, ensuring the cold chain is maintained throughout transit.

What are the payment terms?

We operate on a Quote-to-Invoice basis. A 50% advance is required to lock in the allocation and pricing, with the balance due before dispatch.
