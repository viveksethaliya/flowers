About Fleur Vine: The Professional Floral Partner

Our Identity

Fleur Vine is a specialized floral procurement partner dedicated exclusively to the event management and decor industry. Unlike traditional florists, we operate on a strictly B2B model, stripping away the overhead and distractions of retail to focus on one thing: the reliable supply of high-volume, export-quality fresh flowers.

The Backbone of Your Events

We understand that for an event manager, flowers aren't just "gifts"—they are critical infrastructure for your design. A delay or a drop in quality can compromise your entire production. That is why Fleur Vine has built a supply chain designed for professionals. We source directly from the finest growers to ensure that when our blooms reach your venue, they possess the structural integrity and freshness required for large-scale installations.

Why Professionals Choose Fleur Vine

  • Exclusively B2B: We don't sell to individuals. Every stem we source is reserved for our professional partners, ensuring you always have priority access to the best stock.
  • Volume Capacity: Whether it is a corporate gala or a multi-day destination wedding, we specialize in high-stem-count fulfillment without compromising on grade or consistency.
  • Quality Control: Our flowers undergo a rigorous selection process. We provide the "Export Grade" standard—sturdy stems, vibrant heads, and uniform coloring—designed to withstand the rigors of event environments.

Direct Business Model

To maintain the highest level of security for large-scale transactions, we operate on a Quote-to-Invoice basis. By removing automated payment portals, we ensure a human-to-human verification of every order, providing the personalized service and financial transparency that high-value B2B partnerships require.
