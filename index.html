How to Order: Our B2B Procurement Process

Since we deal with high-volume shipments and large-scale floral contracts, we prioritize financial security and order precision over automated checkouts. Our four-step process ensures you get exactly what your event requires.

1. Browse & Build

Explore our extensive catalog of local and international blooms. Use the "Add to Enquiry List" button to build a list of the specific varieties and stem types required for your project. There are no upfront costs at this stage.

2. Submit Your Enquiry

Once your list is ready, review it in your "Enquiry List" and click the WhatsApp button. This sends your specific requirement directly to our procurement desk. No accounts or login required‚Äîjust direct business communication.

3. Quote & Market Confirmation

Flower pricing fluctuates based on seasonality and market availability. Within a short window, our team will provide a formal bulk-rate quote based on the current best market pricing for your specific volume and event dates.

4. Professional Fulfillment

Upon your approval of the quote, we issue a professional business invoice. To ensure the security of large-sum transactions, payments are handled via Direct Bank Transfer (IMPS/NEFT/RTGS). Once confirmed, your flowers are harvested, packed, and dispatched via your chosen logistics directly to your venue.

Why we don't use an automated payment portal:

  • Security: We handle large-scale business transactions that exceed standard consumer portal limits.
  • Market Pricing: We ensure you get the most competitive wholesale rates on the day of your enquiry, rather than fixed (and often higher) retail prices.
  • Order Accuracy: Every event is unique. A human review of your order ensures we have the stock and logistics capacity to meet your deadline perfectly.
