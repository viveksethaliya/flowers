About Us

Your Professional Partner

The Entity

Fleur Vine is a B2B floral procurement partner for the event management and décor industry. We focus exclusively on high-volume requirements, consistent quality, and dependable delivery for weddings and large events.

Why Professionals Choose Us

  • Exclusively B2B: We don't sell to individuals. Priority access for partners.
  • Volume Capacity: Specialized in high-stem-count fulfillment.
  • Quality Control: "Export Grade" standard—sturdy stems, vibrant heads.
"You manage the event. We supply the flowers."

Direct Business Model

To maintain security for large-scale transactions, we operate on a Quote-to-Invoice basis. Human verification for every order ensures transparency.

Our Commitment

Mission & Values

How We Work

The Farm-to-Venue Process

A streamlined supply chain engineered specifically for high-volume event demands.

1

Consultation & Allocation

We review your mood boards and stem requirements. Flowers are then pre-allocated directly from our network of growers based on your event date.

2

Precision Harvesting

Blooms are cut at the precise maturity stage to ensure they open beautifully right in time for your event.

3

Quality Control & Hydration

Every stem is inspected for A-grade quality. Stems are given immediate hydration treatments before being packed into B2B corrugated boxes.

4

Cold-Chain Dispatch

Your order is transported via specialized temperature-controlled air or road transit, directly to your venue or workshop.
